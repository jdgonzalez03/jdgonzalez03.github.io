# Astro-CV-Skeleton

This is a free and easy-to-use template for creating your resume with a clean and professional design. Simply fill in your details and you're ready to go!

### About the template:
This template was created by [mmouzo](https://github.com/mmouzo). I'm currently utilizing this template for my portfolio due to its simplicity and professional design. However, my ultimate goal is to develop my own custom design to better reflect my unique style and personality.
